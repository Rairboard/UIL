The UILCS_Programming_DataFiles folder for your contest contains the following items:

1. StudentData folder – the sample data files for teams to use during the contest. Do not distribute to teams until the two-hour contest time begins.


~ The items listed below may not be distributed to teams until after the meet, when other confidential materials are returned to schools.~

2. JudgeData and JudgeOutput folders – contain the test data needed for judging.

3. Solutions folder – contains sample solutions to illustrate one possible way to solve each problem.

4. Solutions_JudgeData_OutFiles folder – for convenience, a combination of 2 and 3 above.
